Pro-Sports Outlook


Pro-Sports Outlook is a data visualization project displaying information from professional sports teams from the same city.
Each bubble respresents either a NBA or NFL team from a specific city.
Bubble sizes are based off of team valuation in billions.
All team nodes are interactive and contain more info pertaining to that particular team.
